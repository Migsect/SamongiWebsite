Working in the Product Validation department of the Lifecycle Collaboration organization, I got the opportunity to work with Siemens PLM's Teamcenter software. During this COOP term I learned the basics and importance of testing as well as how to execute, write, and update tests. The term also introduced me to the concept of Software Engineering as seperate from Computer Science as a subject.
Furthermore during this COOP I have learned how to ask good questions to coworkers. At the beginning of the term when I was asking questions I didn't provide information that was useful to the person my questions were directed at. Now when I ask a question I make sure to include enough information such that the person receiving the question is able to formulate a response.
